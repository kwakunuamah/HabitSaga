Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

When you use Habit Chronicle, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account
  • Goal Data: The habits and goals you create, including descriptions and themes
  • Check-in Data: Your daily progress entries and notes
  • Photos or Videos: Avatar images you upload for personalization
  • Diagnostics: Crash reports and performance data to improve the app

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize your habit tracking experience
  • Generate visual story content based on your progress
  • Send you optional reminders and notifications
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Improve app performance and fix issues

Third-Party Services

We work with third-party service providers to deliver our services:

  • Third-party AI services for generating story illustrations
  • Backend and cloud infrastructure providers for data storage
  • Subscription and payment processors
  • Crash reporting and diagnostics tools

Data Security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and export your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of optional communications
